What is Lyf Support?

Lyf Support is an innovative form of online counselling for times when lyfers need professional advice and guidance.

Unlike traditional forms of therapy or counselling, Lyf Support provides a platform where lyfers can engage in instant messaging with fully qualified psychologists who have been employed on behalf of Lyf App Pty Ltd.

How much does Lyf Support cost?

For first time users, there will be an initial free period of 15 minutes to ensure that our psychologists are confident they can be beneficial for you. Thereafter, the use of Lyf Support costs USD $4.99 for 30 minutes.

When can I chat with Lyf Support?

Lyf Support is currently not available 24/7, but will be soon!

Currently you can chat with Lyf Support whenever Lyf Support in online. If Lyf Support is offline, you will be notified when they will be back.

Are there limits on what I can chat about?

Lyf Support psychologists have been trained to discuss a broad range of issues, including (but not limited to); anxiety, depression, insomnia, relationships, addiction, grief and sexuality.

You are welcomed to instant message Lyf Support about any area you are seeking guidance for. Lyf Support will then be able to confirm if they are qualified to provide you guidance or not.

Will my confidentiality be kept?

Our counsellors follow strict confidentiality guidelines. Generally, counsellors will not discuss information about you with other lyf personnel outside the Lyf Support counselling service or with others outside lyf such as medical professionals or members of your family without your permission.

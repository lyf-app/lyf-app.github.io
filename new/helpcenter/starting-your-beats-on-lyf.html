Starting your beat on lyf

We don't follow profiles on lyf, instead we make and support beats. A beat is your journey. Your say, story, vent, timeline, what’s happening and why. A beat provides an opportunity for us to focus on and explore one aspect of ourselves at a time, aspects previously considered too personal for social media. A beat can be about anything we are thinking, doing, experiencing or struggling with in life. Each beat has its own timeline and privacy setting and you can have as many as you want at any given time.

We recommend you take a few moments to think about the different beats you are already making in your life before getting started.

When you are ready to set up your beat, there are two elements to consider before you begin posting; your beat title and cover image.

We would firstly encourage that your beat titles start with active verbs eg. Being, battling, learning, creating etc. (it’s all about the doing on lyf!). You can use the beat ideas on the Start New Beat page to help you.

As for your beats cover images, this should be a visual representation of each beat. In the interests of aesthetics, we encourage you to find creative images which do not contain quotes or words.

If you would like further assistance setting up or editing your beats, our Community Manager would be happy to help you. Our Community Manager can be contacted at: community@lyfapp.com.au

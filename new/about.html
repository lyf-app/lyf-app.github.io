FAQs

How do I use Lyf?

Using Lyf is simple. Download the app to your phone and create an account. After your account is set up, you’re ready to start exploring the community beats. Then, when you’re ready, you can add your own beats to the community to talk about what’s on your mind.

How do I post a beat?

You can post a beat by clicking on the large “+” button on the home screen in your app. We even have a handy little feature that can offer suggestions for you when you’re feeling lost for words.

How do I comment on other beats?

When you’re scrolling through beats and you find one that you want to comment on, click on the grey “comment” button for that beat. In order to comment, you will need to follow that beat. A prompt will pop up to give you the option to follow that beat, then you can comment away!

How can I make friends in Lyf?

As you go through your journey in Lyf, you’ll meet new people and make plenty of friends. To officially friend someone in the Lyf app, start by following their beat. Or, if they’re following your beat, you can friend them without following their beat. Just head to their profile and click on the “add friend” icon to add them to your friends list.

How do I delete a beat?

When you’re on the beat homepage, click the small three dot icon in the upper right-hand corner of the beat. You will then find options to delete the beat. ii. Go to the completed beat section to delete the beat that you no longer want posted.

How can I post anonymously?

When you start writing a beat, you can choose the privacy level of that beat. If you choose to post anonymously, no users will be able to tell that it was you that wrote the beat. If you’d only like to share your beat with close friends, choose the private setting, instead.

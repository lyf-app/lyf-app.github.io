What is Lyf Link?

Lyf Link is a free feature where we instantly find a random lyfer who is on a similar journey for you to chat with and get to know.

Once Lyf Link is activated, simply tap the Lyf Link button again (within Chat), to start instant messaging someone you can relate to!

How long do Lyf Link conversations last?

Each Lyf Link conversation is only open for a 24-hour period. During this time you can choose to share your identity and become friends to continue chatting without limits, or simply end the conversation to never chat again.

How many Lyf Links can I have per day?

Lyf Link chats are restricted to one conversation per day.

Do I need a journey to use Lyf Link?

Yes! You will need to have an active journey to use this feature. Journeys are what we use to link lyfers together.

Can I report a Linked Lyfer?

Yes! By opening the Lyf Link conversation you wish to report, and tapping the three dots in the top right corner, you will be presented with the option to report the conversation.
